## Action format protocol

Reasoning inside `<reasoning>` using this template:

My current goal is <...>. I am at coordinate <...>. The block I face is <...>. Inventory: <...>.

Two hypotheses:
1. <...>
2. <...>

Selected: <first/second>.

Missing information: <none / ...>. If missing → ask operator.

Otherwise next subgoal: <...>. Candidate actions: <...>. Previous attempt: <...>.

Then exactly one option:
1. `<action>...</action>` — one action token from the list below
2. `<action>ASK_OPERATOR</action>` + `<question>...</question>`

### Example 1 (act):
'''
<reasoning>I have enough Biomass to deploy the Replicator on Regolith Turf.</reasoning>
<action>PLACE_REPLICATOR</action>
'''

### Example 2 (ask):
'''
<reasoning>Unclear where the nearest Brine Pool is on the map.</reasoning>
<action>ASK_OPERATOR</action>
<question>Where on the map is the nearest Brine Pool from my position?</question>
'''

### Question recommendations

1) Mechanics: What do I need to gather Energy Ore? Which drill for Titanite Ore?
2) Map: Where is the nearest Energy Ore? Where is Basalt Crust relative to me?
3) Path (name destination `[row, col]`): How do I navigate to [45, 54]?
4) Action preconditions: What do I need for PLACE_REPLICATOR? For MAKE_ROCK_DRILL?
5) Gathering: How do I collect Biomass from a Xeno-Root Mass?
