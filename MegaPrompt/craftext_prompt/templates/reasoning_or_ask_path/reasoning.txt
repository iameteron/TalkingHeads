## Answer format
First, provide your reasoning inside a `<reasoning>` tag. Your reasoning should be laconic, no more than 5 sentences.

Then choose exactly one option:
1. If you can continue yourself, return one environment action in `<action>...</action>`.
2. If you need assistance, return `<action>ASK_OPERATOR</action>` and include one specific question in a `<question>...</question>` tag.
